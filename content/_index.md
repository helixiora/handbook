Use this handbook whenever you are unsure how something works in practice at Helixiora.

- If you are **new**, read [Your First Day](/docs/first-day/) and [Welcome](/docs/welcome/) first.
- For **day-to-day work**, check [How We Work](/docs/how-we-work/), [AI & Experimentation](/docs/ai-and-experimentation/), and [Communication & Meetings](/docs/communication-and-meetings/).
- For **time off, money, and travel**, use [Time Off & Holidays](/docs/time-off/), [Compensation & Benefits](/docs/compensation-and-benefits/), [Expenses](/docs/expenses/), [Travel & Helixiora Connect](/docs/travel-and-helixiora-connect/), and [Helixiora Connect](/docs/helixiora-connect/).
- For **tools and security**, keep [Tools & Support](/docs/tools-and-support/) and [Security & Data Handling](/docs/security-and-data-handling/) close.

If something in the handbook ever conflicts with your contract or local law, your contract and applicable law take precedence. When that happens, tell the handbook owners so we can fix the page.
