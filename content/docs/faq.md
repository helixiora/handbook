## What this page is for

This page gives short answers to frequent handbook questions and points you to the full policy pages.

## What you should do

Use this page for quick triage, then follow linked pages for full process detail.

### Where do I ask about payslips?

Use the payroll support route described in [Compensation & Benefits](/docs/compensation-and-benefits/).

### How do I book leave?

Ask Robin, then keep the absence visible in your calendar and Slack as described in [Time Off & Holidays](/docs/time-off/).

### What if I am sick?

Follow the same-day process in [Time Off & Holidays](/docs/time-off/).

### Can I buy software myself?

Ask Robin or Walter for approval first, then follow [Expenses](/docs/expenses/).

### What happens at Helixiora Connect?

See [Helixiora Connect](/docs/helixiora-connect/).

### Can I expense AI tools or token usage?

Usually yes, if the usage is reasonable and helps you do better work. If you need budget, access, or you are unsure about cost, discuss it with Walter first as described in [AI & Experimentation](/docs/ai-and-experimentation/).

### Which tools are mandatory on day one?

See [Your First Day](/docs/first-day/) and [Tools & Support](/docs/tools-and-support/).
