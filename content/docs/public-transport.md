## What we cover

This sample policy assumes Helixiora supports commuting primarily through public transport.

| Travel mode | Sample support |
| --- | --- |
| Train, tram, metro, bus | Covered through a company mobility card or reimbursement |
| Bicycle commuting | Optional per-kilometer allowance |
| Car travel | Only when public transport is impractical or pre-approved |
| Taxi | Exception-based, typically for late travel or accessibility needs |

If Helixiora offers a specific mobility product such as an `NS Business Card`, name it here directly.

## How to arrange your commute

New starters should set up their commuting option during the first week.

1. Open the `HR Portal`.
2. Go to `Mobility` or `Travel Benefits`.
3. Choose your primary commuting method.
4. Submit any required home-to-office details.
5. Wait for confirmation before assuming reimbursement is active.

## Occasional business travel

Travel outside your normal commute should usually be booked or approved ahead of time.

- Use public transport by default for domestic travel.
- Book higher-cost options only when timing or accessibility justifies it.
- Attach the ticket or booking receipt to the relevant expense claim if needed.

## Practical expectations

- Keep your commuting method up to date if your work pattern changes.
- Do not submit your normal commute twice through both the mobility benefit and the expense tool.
- Ask before booking hotels, taxis, or premium rail options.

The goal is predictable reimbursement, not case-by-case guesswork.
