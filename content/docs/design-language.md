## Brand translation

The handbook does not try to mimic a slide deck. It translates the brand into a web-native system.

From the cover of the 2024 brand guide, the dominant visual cues are:

| Element | Applied on the site |
| --- | --- |
| Deep navy field | Hero panels, footer, buttons, and headings |
| Bright white logo treatment | High-contrast typography and glass surfaces |
| Gold circuit motif | Accent lines, section labels, hover states, and hero artwork |
| Lato Bold and Regular | Display and body text choices |

## Web-specific interpretation

The site adds a few things the PDF cannot provide on its own:

- Responsive spacing for mobile and desktop.
- Soft glass surfaces to keep the layout light instead of heavy.
- Scroll-friendly article pages with a table of contents.
- Motion limited to hover and page-load transitions.

## What to preserve

If this site evolves, keep these rules intact:

1. Let navy carry the authority.
2. Use gold as emphasis, not as a flood color.
3. Keep the navigation restrained.
4. Make typography and spacing do most of the work.

That balance keeps the site feeling modern and sleek without drifting away from the Helixiora identity.
