## What this page is for

This page provides a readable overview of behavior and conduct expectations for everyone at Helixiora, and should be read alongside [Communication & Meetings](/docs/communication-and-meetings/).

## What you should do

Know and follow policies on:

- Code of conduct and respectful communication
- Equal opportunity and anti-harassment
- Conflicts of interest and disclosure expectations
- Grievance and escalation pathways
- Whistleblowing route (if applicable)

## Who owns or approves it

People Operations owns this content and escalation process. Leadership approves policy direction and accountability standards.

## Where to go in the tool stack

- `HR Portal` for policy acknowledgements and guidance links
- Designated reporting channels for formal concerns

## What happens if something goes wrong

If you experience or witness policy violations, document what happened and report through the appropriate channel. Reports are handled with confidentiality to the extent possible and non-retaliation expectations apply.
