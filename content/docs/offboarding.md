## What this page is for

This page explains the standard exit process when someone leaves Helixiora.

## What you should do

- Confirm resignation in writing and review notice period obligations.
- Build a practical handover plan for active work.
- Return company equipment and revoke local copies of company data.
- Submit final expenses and complete final administrative tasks.
- Coordinate access removal timing with IT and your manager.

## Who owns or approves it

Managers and founders own process steps. Managers own delivery handover. IT owns access revocation and device intake.

## Where to go in the tool stack

- `HR Portal` for exit documentation
- `Expense Portal` for final claims
- `IT Helpdesk` for equipment and access checklist

## What happens if something goes wrong

If access, payroll, or handover steps are incomplete, raise it immediately with your manager and founders to avoid security, compliance, or payment delays.
