## What this page is for

This page explains what Helixiora Connect is, why it matters, and how to handle planning, travel, and expenses for it.

## What you should do

- Treat Helixiora Connect as an important company moment, not an optional afterthought.
- Plan travel early and use reasonable booking choices.
- Follow the normal expense route for your contract form and keep receipts.
- Discuss hotels, flights, or other costs over EUR 200 with your manager in advance where possible.
- Tell your manager early if something may affect your attendance or travel plan.

## What Helixiora Connect is

Helixiora Connect is our quarterly off-site.

It exists to give the team time together in person for things that work better face to face, such as:

- company alignment
- working sessions
- planning and discussion
- team connection

For a remote-first company, this is part of how we stay coherent as a team instead of becoming a loose collection of individual contributors.

## Standard three-day format

Helixiora Connect normally runs across three days:

### Day 1: Founder day

The founders review every part of the business. The goal is to create a clear shared picture of what is happening across Helixiora, what is working, what is not, and where attention is needed.

### Day 2: Team day

The team discusses that review, adds more input, challenges assumptions where needed, and helps shape the plan for the next quarter.

### Day 3: Fun day

The last day is for a bonding activity. This is deliberate, not filler. Stronger working relationships make the rest of the quarter function better.

## Cost coverage

Helixiora Connect is fully covered by the company.

That includes reasonable event-related travel and stay costs, but the same judgment rules still apply:

- flights are economy
- public transport in the Netherlands is normally second class
- if trains are especially busy, first class is acceptable
- hotels and taxis should be reasonable for the situation

If you are unsure whether a booking is reasonable, ask before you book it.

## Travel and expense handling

Use the normal expense process for your contract form:

- Deel if your setup uses Deel
- otherwise the email route that matches your contract form

For the broader travel rules, also read [Travel & Helixiora Connect](/docs/travel-and-helixiora-connect/) and [Expenses](/docs/expenses/).

## Who owns or approves it

Founders and managers own Helixiora Connect planning and expectations. Your manager is the first point of contact for attendance, booking questions, and higher-cost exceptions.

## Where to go in the tool stack

- Calendar for event timing and travel planning
- Your manager for approval questions or exceptions
- Deel or email for expenses, depending on your contract form

## What happens if something goes wrong

If travel changes, costs increase, or you may not be able to attend, raise it early with your manager so the plan can be adjusted without last-minute surprises.
